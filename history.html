History Of Paris

Paris, known as the "City of Light", is more than 2,000 years old. The Gauls of the Parisii tribe settled there between 250 and 200 BC and founded a fishing village on an island in the river that is the present-day Ile de la Cité -- the center around which Paris developed.

  • 52 BC Paris is founded when the Parisii tribe settles on the Ile de la Cité.
  • 3rd Century The Romans establish a town on the Left Bank, calling it Lutetia.
  • 508 Clovis the Frank makes Paris his capital.
  • 12th Century Notre-Dame Cathedral construction begins.
  • 1789 The French Revolution begins in Paris with the storming of the Bastille.
  • 1889 The Eiffel Tower is completed for the World's Fair.
  • 1940-1944 Paris is occupied by the Germans during WWII.
  • Present Paris is one of the world's leading centers for arts, culture, fashion, and academia.
